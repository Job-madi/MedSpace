About MED.SPACE

Med.Space is a web application where medical professionals
from all over the world can get opinions from other verified medical professionals.

Why Med.Space?

Recent studies of medical errors have estimated errors may account for as many as 251,000 deaths annually in the United States (U.S)., making medical errors the third leading cause of death.
Med.Space solves this issue by allowing doctors to get a 2nd opion from other medical professionals at the tips of their hands for free
